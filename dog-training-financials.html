Dog Training & Mission Program Financials

This page breaks down the cost structure, estimated revenue, and nonprofit-aligned programming tied to our dog training efforts and rescue mission.

Nonprofit-Aligned Services

  • Rescue & Rehoming: $800–$1,500 per non-working dog (food, vet, foster/adoption coordination)
  • Free/Low-Cost Training: Offered to veterans, diabetics, PTSD survivors (grant-supported)
  • Education Outreach: Dog enrichment classes, therapy visits, mental health seminars

Mission Statement Reminder

We are committed to reinvesting profits from our café operations to subsidize dog training and adoption for people in need. Not every pup needs a job to be part of the mission.
