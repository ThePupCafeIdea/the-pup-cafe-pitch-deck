ThePupCafeIdea@Gmail.com | @ThePupCafeIdea

The Problem

We live in a system that undervalues care: for animals, for workers, and for each other. Loneliness is rising. Dogs are abandoned. Café workers live on poverty wages.

The Pup Café Solution

Not a novelty — a full-system alternative built around rescue, dignity, and worker care.

🐾 Immediate Impact

  • Reduces pressure on kill shelters through direct placement & dog matching
  • Creates real worker careers with built-in pay equity, not tip dependency
  • Provides enrichment, rest, and rotation time for dogs — not performative presence
  • Dignity is built in — no underpaid roles, no burnout-as-default

🌟 Short-Term Goals

  • Monthly adoption events with municipal + rescue partners
  • Popups with local vendors, healers, and marginalized creatives
  • Mutual aid fridge with café excess + dog supplies
  • Community-led events and uses — care replaces consumption

🌱 Long-Term Vision

  • Outreach + rescue logistics
  • Service dog training & community education
  • Career pipeline for dog handling + care
  • Toolkit for replicating the model

🌟 Possible Solutions

  • Train dogs for PTSD, diabetes alerting, or ESA work — sold below market cost
  • If a dog isn’t a training fit, we find them a loving home
  • Worker co-design and feedback baked into operations
  • Community space that actively replaces isolation with engagement
