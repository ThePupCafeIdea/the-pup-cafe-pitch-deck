The Pup Café

Coffee. Community. Care.

A worker-first, dog-centered café where rescue meets dignity.

Hunter Harris | ThePupCafeIdea@gmail.com | Dallas, TX

The Problem

Every day, incredible rescue dogs sit in shelters — unseen, under-socialized, and forgotten.

Meanwhile, service workers in cafés are underpaid and burned out. Most businesses hoard value at the top.

We believe business should feel like care — not extraction.

The Solution

The Pup Café is a modern, ethical café that puts rescue dogs and workers first.

Dogs get safety, care, and a path to adoption. Workers earn real wages and share in profits. Customers become part of a joyful mission just by showing up.

Shared Value Model

  • Dogs = co-beneficiaries, not decoration
  • Workers = profit share + wage
  • Community = joy through ethical business

Every cup of coffee supports someone who deserves more.

Ethical Profit Framework

  • 40–50% → Dog Rescue, Training, & Care
  • 25–30% → Worker Profit Sharing
  • 10–15% → Social Good Reinvestment
  • Up to 5% → Capped Founder Draw

These are target percentages — flexible as we grow.

“DALL·E” Dog System

  • Greeter Dogs – love attention
  • Chill Dogs – cozy background energy
  • Service Trainees – future heroes
  • Off-Duty Dogs – resting, unseen, cared for

All roles are voluntary, safe, and dignified.

Startup Plan

  1. Community building + outreach
  2. Pop-up pilot events
  3. First lease & soft launch (2025)
  4. Full Pup Café model with dogs + staff

Startup Cost Estimates

  • $15–25k – Mobile or pop-up pilot
  • $50–75k – Small leased café
  • $100–150k+ – Full Pup Café dream

What We Need

  • Legal & ethical advisors
  • Rescue org & behaviorist partners
  • Funding (grants, donors, aligned backers)
  • Creative allies: design, social, story

We're not asking for ROI. We're building shared joy.

Let’s Build Something Beautiful

A café where dogs, workers, and communities are treated with care — not as costs.
