The Pup Café

Coffee. Community. Care.

A worker-first, dog-centered café where rescue meets dignity.

Hunter Harris | ThePupCafeIdea@gmail.com | Dallas, TX

The Problem

Every day, incredible rescue dogs sit in shelters — unseen, under-socialized, and often forgotten. Meanwhile, service workers in cafés and small shops are paid barely enough to survive, with no path to ownership or shared success.

Traditional businesses hoard value at the top. Dogs become decoration. Workers become replaceable. And cafés become just another transaction space.

We believe business should feel like care — not extraction.

The Solution

The Pup Café is a modern, mission-first café that puts dogs and workers at the center. Rescue dogs live and thrive in a certified safe café space, socializing and resting based on their needs — not performance.

Workers receive livable wages and a share in profits. Customers enjoy great coffee while contributing to a system rooted in mutual aid and joy.

It’s more than a café — it’s an ethical ecosystem.

The Model: Shared Value

  • Dogs are treated as co-beneficiaries — not props.
  • Workers earn more than wages — they earn value.
  • Profits are shared between dogs, humans, and future good.

Every cup of coffee supports someone who deserves more.

Our Ethical Profit Framework

  • 40–50% → Dog-Focused Initiatives
    Rescue, medical care, enrichment, adoption & training
  • 25–30% → Worker Profit Sharing
    On top of hourly wages or salary
  • 10–15% → Other Social Good Investments
    Co-ops, shelters, mutual aid orgs
  • Up to 5% → Capped Founder Draw
    Max $60–100k/year — no unlimited upside

These are our goals — not our starting point. We'll scale toward this model over time.

The People & Roles

  • Founder: Vision, systems, long-term stewardship
  • Workers: Baristas, dog handlers, café team — paid & profit-sharing
  • Rescue Orgs: Provide adoptable dogs & support placements
  • Trainers / Behaviorists: Ensure dogs thrive with ethical enrichment
  • Customers: Supporters, friends, co-creators of this space

This is a space built *with* people, not just *for* them.

The Dog Model: “DALL·E” System

  • Greeter Dogs: Friendly pups who love interaction
  • Chill Dogs: Lounge in cozy zones, just being present
  • Service Dog Trainees: Long-term guests preparing for real placements
  • Off-Duty Dogs: Fully cared for, not visible to the public

Every dog rotates, every dog rests, every dog is treated with dignity.

Startup Phases

  1. Community Building: Outreach, branding, local awareness
  2. Pop-Up Pilot: Farmers market / mobile test runs
  3. Soft Launch: Lease secured, first café open, Dallas 2025
  4. Full Launch: Team scaling, full dog-care ecosystem live

Every phase is rooted in ethical planning and transparency.

Startup Cost Estimates

  • $15–25k: Bare bones test location / pop-up cart
  • $50–75k: Lease + small café buildout
  • $100–150k: Dream launch (patio yard, staff, full system)

No luxury budgets. Just enough to build something beautiful.
