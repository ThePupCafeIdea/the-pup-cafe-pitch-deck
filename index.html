Our Mission & Model

This isn't charity. This is shared survival. We split our net profits intentionally:

🐕 50%
to rescue, rehab & rehome dogs
👩‍🍳 30%
directly to café workers
🌱 10–15%
community care + events
🧍 1%
to founder (just enough to live)
