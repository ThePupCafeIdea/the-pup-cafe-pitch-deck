Our Mission & Values

At The Pup Café, we are driven by:

  • Joy: Creating a joyful environment for dogs, workers, and patrons alike.
  • Dignity: Ensuring fair treatment and respect for all beings involved.
  • Solidarity: Building a community that supports each other.
  • Care: Prioritizing the well-being of our dogs and staff.

Our mission is to create a space where rescue dogs find loving homes, workers are valued and empowered, and the community comes together over great coffee.

The Problem

Every year, millions of rescue dogs remain in shelters, longing for companionship. Simultaneously, service industry workers often face undervaluation and burnout. Communities lack spaces that foster genuine connection and mutual support.

The Solution

The Pup Café offers a harmonious solution by:

  • Providing a nurturing environment for rescue dogs to interact with potential adopters.
  • Ensuring fair wages and profit-sharing for our dedicated workers.
  • Creating a welcoming space for the community to connect and support a meaningful cause.

Who’s Building This?

Hi — I’m Hunter Harris. I’m not a CEO. I’m not a business expert. I’m a person with a vision, a dog-shaped hole in my heart, and a need to build something gentler than the system we’re in.

This project is my full-time life’s work. I’m learning as I go, reaching out constantly, and asking for help wherever I can find it.

I believe in collective ownership, mutual aid, and ethical labor. I’m not building this to scale endlessly — I’m building it so others can live and work with dignity. Me included.
