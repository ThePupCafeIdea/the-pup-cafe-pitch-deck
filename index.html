🐾 The Pup Café 🐾

A café for dogs, workers & mutual care

Created by Hunter Harris & Stephen Barksdale

Contact: ThePupCafeIdea@Gmail.com | @ThePupCafeIdea

The Problem

Our current system undervalues care for animals, workers, and community.

  • 🐶 3.1M dogs enter US shelters annually
  • 👩‍🍳 52% of café workers earn below living wage
  • 🏘️ 61% of Gen Z feel lonely weekly

The Pup Café Solution

A full-system alternative built around rescue, dignity, and worker care.

  • 🐾 Immediate Impact: Lessens pressure on kill shelters, provides real worker careers, and ensures daily enrichment for dogs.
  • 🌟 Short-Term Goals: Monthly adoption events, popups with local vendors, and a mutual aid fridge.
  • 🌱 Long-Term Vision: Flexible profit-sharing, outreach network, and a toolkit to replicate the model.

What It Takes to Start

Mapping the path to launch with flexible tiers reflecting real costs and growing community care.

A Day in the Life

A senior dog naps under a warm window. A barista makes pour-over while chatting with a local artist. Someone drops $5 in the donation jar for vet bills. The air smells like fresh cinnamon and care.

Note: In compliance with Texas law, dogs are welcome in designated outdoor dining areas only.

Where Revenue Comes From

  • ☕ Coffee + Food: 60 customers/day × $7 avg ticket
  • 🐾 Dog Events: Weekly socials, training clinics, adoption popups
    • 💖 Community Support: Donation jars, online tips, vet fundraisers
  • 👕 Merch & Media: Mugs, shirts, zines, art prints
  • 📦 Future Add-ons: Dog day-care, training programs, educational workshops

What Impact Looks Like

Every dollar turns into tangible results:

  • 🐶 70+ dogs rescued in first 18 months
  • 👩‍🍳 5+ fair-wage jobs created
  • 🧺 Monthly mutual aid efforts: food, pet pantry, free vet days
  • 🎤 25+ community events and storytelling nights

Where This Grows From Here

This is a prototype — not a one-off.

  • Year 1: Launch in Dallas. Measure, refine, improve.
  • Year 2–3: Add dog training, storytelling, nonprofit wing.
  • Year 4+: Replication kit. Start transition to co-op governance.

This Isn’t Built Alone

We don’t need a visionary founder. We need everyday people who know care is worth building.

  • Have you worked in food service? Rescued a dog?
  • Do you believe mutual aid beats burnout?
  • Do you want to help build something slow, kind, and real?
