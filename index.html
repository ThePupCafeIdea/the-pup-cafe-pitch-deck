The Pup Café

Coffee. Community. Care.

A worker-first, dog-centered café where rescue meets dignity.

Hunter Harris | ThePupCafeIdea@gmail.com | Dallas, TX

The Problem

Rescue dogs go unseen and unadopted. Workers in food service are underpaid and burned out. Most businesses hoard value rather than sharing it with those who create it.

The Solution

A modern café that creates joy, shares profits, gives dogs safe jobs, and makes customers part of a mission just by showing up.

The Model: Shared Value

  • Dogs receive daily care and a path to adoption
  • Workers are paid fairly + share in profits
  • Profits support dogs, people, and ethical causes

Profit Framework

  • 40–50% → Dog Care & Adoption Support
  • 25–30% → Worker Profit Sharing
  • 10–15% → Social Good Funding
  • Up to 5% → Capped Founder Draw

*These reflect our values — not our startup reality.*

What We Need

  • Ethical funders & partners
  • Legal & structural advice
  • Rescue, vet, and dog behavior collaborators
  • Supporters, creatives, builders, believers

Let’s Build Something Beautiful

A café where dignity and coffee are served daily. For dogs. For workers. For the community.
