Dog Training & Nonprofit Financial Evaluation

Our dog training programs are designed to offer high-quality training at subsidized rates. This section breaks down the costs of training, rehoming, and educational outreach for non-working dogs, as well as the social benefits generated by these initiatives.

Program Area Cost / Investment Revenue / Impact Assumptions
Dog Training $2,500–$6,000 per dog $1,000–$3,500 adoption fee (subsidized) Includes trainer wages, housing, food, and veterinary care
Working Dog Value – $10,000–$50,000 market value* Our sales are set below market value to maximize social impact
Rescue & Rehoming $800–$1,500 per dog Nonprofit-supported Costs cover medical, foster care, and adoption coordination
Free/Low-Cost Programs Staff, space, and program costs Enhanced community outreach Focus on vulnerable groups: veterans, PTSD, diabetics, etc.

*Market values reflect the cost of fully trained service dogs. Our approach is mission-driven: not every dog is expected to "earn" its keep in the traditional sense.
