🐾 The Pup Café 🐾

A Café Redefining Care for Dogs, Workers & Communities
Created by Hunter Harris & Stephen Barksdale
ThePupCafeIdea@Gmail.com | @ThePupCafeIdea

The Problem

We live in a system that undervalues care: for animals, for workers, and for each other. Loneliness is rising, dogs are abandoned, and café workers live on poverty wages.

  • 🐶 670,000 dogs euthanized annually — many could become service animals.
  • 💼 52% of café workers earn below a living wage.
  • 😔 61% of Gen Z report feeling lonely weekly.

The Solution

We’re creating a new kind of café where care is at the center of everything — for dogs, workers, and the community.

  • 🐾 Rescue, train, and place dogs at risk of euthanasia.
  • 💼 Create fair-wage, equitable jobs for café staff.
  • 🌱 Use café profits to fund community support programs.

Revenue Model

The Pup Café isn’t just a café — it’s an ecosystem of care:

  • ☕ Coffee & Food: 60 customers/day × $7 avg ticket
  • 🐾 Dog Events: Adoption popups, training clinics
  • 👕 Merch & Media: Branded goods, art, zines
  • 🎓 Training & Placement: Service dog training and adoption
  • 💖 Community Support: Donation jars, pay-it-forward programs
